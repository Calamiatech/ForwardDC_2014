Amongst the NES Cartridges & Flea Markets, a Crate Digga emerged from his West Baltimore Basement...

Beneath the sped-up drum breaks, Nick tha 1da began training in various Beat Battles/Showcases [Beat Grinder/Red Bull Big Tune/The Beatdown/Istandard/Etc] while simultaneously searching for the sampled originals to his favorite Hip-hop classics. While diggin thru the world's crates, Nick has hosted Loop Diggin 101[WMUC 88.1 FM], produced the OST for the film Loose Change 9/11 & various artists including: Tanya Morgan, Raheem Devaughn, Tom Hardy, Grap Luva, Kenn Starr & Mas...
