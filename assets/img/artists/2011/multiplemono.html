Multiple Mono

AFP
http://www.soundcloud.com/multiplemono

Multiple Mono creates engaging, experimental music using a balanced blend of twentieth-century instruments and twenty-first-century technology.  The resulting electroacoustic soundscapes range from danceable to ambient, aggressive to ethereal, with songwriting that utilizes found sound to emphasize the emotion of its composers.  For live performances, Multiple Mono filters these elements through the collective energy of both their listeners and themselves to reconstruct these sounds the way they're meant to be heard at that time, ensuring that each new set is truly that - in the moment, equal parts familiar and innovative, evolving as is only natural for such music to do.

Multiple Mono is:

Rob Paterson - laptop/real time audio manipulation

Colin Kasprowicz - electric & electronic bass/synthesizers
