Aaron Clark

HUMANAUT
http://www.humanaut.net

Hailing from the Steel City of Pittsburgh, Aaron is a co-founder of the Humanaut techno / house collective and a primary member of the VIA Music & New Media Festival team. As a DJ, Aaron consistently refines a unique and energetic sound which makes it enormously difficult to pigeon hole. Depending on when and where he is in performance, you can expect to hear a wide variety of masterfully programmed house music - from funk-filled and soulful to cutting-edge techno.

As time goes on, he's finding himself more enamored with the behind-the-scenes workings – conceptualizing everything from the Humanaut parties that helped start everything, to larger projects such as VIA and 2010's Centre Street Social after party in Detroit.
