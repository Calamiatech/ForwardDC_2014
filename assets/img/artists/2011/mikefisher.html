Mike Fisher is a Brooklyn transplant originating from Washington, DC. A difficult person to miss in a crowd, Fisher towers over everyone with his tall stature and exuberant character. In his time as a DJ he has played alongside the likes of M.A.N.D.Y., Lee Foss, Jamie Jones, Josh Wink, Lee Curtiss, Ryan Crosson and many more. Along with being reliable for good times, Fisher is known for his hard work in bringing excellent DJ’s to Washington, D.C. as an core member and resident DJ of the Washington, DC Life parties.
