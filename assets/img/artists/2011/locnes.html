Mysterious, elusive, yet always profound when sighted, LOC.NES is the alter ego of beat-maker and LivePA artist Kelvin Gumbs. Hailing from Toronto, Canada LOC.NES deconstructs and transforms yesterday's dusty grooves into dance floor bangers for the new millennium. He carefully combines elements from old vinyl records, House, Hip Hop and Electro into his own brand of body-moving future-beats.

LOC.NES has been producing music under a myriad of aliases since 1996. He began with Hip Hop but gradually expanded his sound to include more elements of electronic music. Not bound by the conventions of any one genre, LOC.NES continually pushes the boundaries of electronic music production and performance by utilizing a combination of old-school hardware and cutting-edge software.

A visionary member of the MonoMachina collective of LivePA artists, LOC.NES will debut a solo project, his album entitled "Adventures In Fantastic Land," in the summer of 2011.
