Aligning Minds

ALEPH ZERO / 88
http://www.aligningminds.com

Ethereal and dubby, uplifting and haunting, Aligning Minds music winds its way through the imaginative souls of its listeners, offering a deep exploration of subsonic mood and emotional atmosphere. Focused on unifying electronic music through diverse influence, the process fuses elements of many genres like dubstep, breakbeat, downtempo, idm/glitch, and techno but without the need for formulaic restrictions.

Through mastering studio skills and live improvisational performances, Aligning Minds has been gaining interest and support from all over the global spectrum of electronic music. The last year alone has brought a 27 city national tour, features on many respectable radio shows including BBC Radio 1's The Mary Ann Hobbs Show, a full length album and a handful of compilation / remix releases. Aligning Minds has had the pleasure of collaborating with and performing alongside artists as varied as Shpongle, Daedelus, Mochipet, Emancipator, Eskmo, John Tejada, Nosaj Thing, Benga, edIT, Kraddy, Jimmy Edgar, Headhunter, Bluetech, Kilowatts, Untold, OTT, Beats Antique, Juno Reactor, Milosh, Alex Grey, and the list continues.

Intent on reaching a musical standpoint of balance and harmony between stylistic ideas, Aligning Minds moves forward as a musical force with an ethic of positive growth and change.
