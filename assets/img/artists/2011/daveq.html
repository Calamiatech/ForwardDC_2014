Dave Q

DUBWAR / TWISUP / THE INDEX
http://www.dubwarnyc.com

Dave Q is a founder and resident DJ of Dub War, as well as the new Brooklyn monthly Twisup, which continues dubstep's evolution into new tempos and textures. His DJ sets are relentlessly forward-thinking explorations of new forms of bass-oriented dance music which are both experimental and dancefloor friendly.

DQ also runs the record label The Index with the New York electronic/dub composer Badawi. The label presents new Badawi productions and collaborations with some of the pioneers of new electronic music, including Shackleton, Headhunter, Spatial, and Vladislav Delay.
