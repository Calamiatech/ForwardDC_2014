Palermo-native Rosario is a man of action. Party sergeant by night, like a dog in heat, he’s never satisfied with a bone that’s thrown at him.

Rosario is an integral part of the DC-nightlife scene. He was a fixture at the notorious afterhours Rent parties and continues to be in popular demand, regularly asked to perform at parties by various promoters.
