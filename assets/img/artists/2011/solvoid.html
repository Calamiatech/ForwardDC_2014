Solvoid
GDG
http://solvoid.com

Somewhere between house, techno, dub and two-step, Solvoid emerges from the primordial nether-regions ensconced in sub-bass and draped in analog hues. The latest side project from North American tech-house icon Noah Pred is an exploration of modern bass music. A fresh take on the various 'steps espoused across the pond, Solvoid merges synthetic shards of melody, undulating pulsations and shuffling, swung rhythms that point directly towards the future.
