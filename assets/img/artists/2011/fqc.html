Hailing from Baltimore, MD, Jason Joseph and Jon Mack have become some of the most trusted names along the East Coast within the underground house community. Their long standing residency at Red Maple has been recognized across the country as a party that's revitilazing the underground scene. For over eight years now, the two have combined their individual talents, musical influences, and passion to unite people through music. Their eclectic range and distinctive mixing abilities have catapulted them into an elite group of true tastemakers. Having played alongside some of the most in demand DJ's/producers in the world and in front of crowds at some of the countries top festivals, ForQuarters Collective is one to watch in 2011!
