Chris Lee (a.k.a. Spankbox) is a Producer, Live PA artist and founder of the Toronto based Electronic Music movement MonoMachina. Attacking his performances with hard-hitting, 4-on-the-floor, minimal-electro beats, Spankbox's pure Live PA sound is shaped and improvised on the fly using a hardware-only approach without the aid of computers or software. Tracks are born and arranged on the fly. The premiere single “Spare Parts” will be released this month from Spankbox’s debut album “The Boy With the Drum Machine Heart ”.

Rooted in electronic music creation for nearly 14 years, Chris began creating electronic music in the mid 90’s while influenced by the immergence of industrial music. His first synthesizer, a Roland MC-303, was a birthday present he received in 1998, which is still part of his live rig to this day. Chris currently uses his organization MonoMachina to promote and showcase Toronto's underground electronic music talent at clubs and venues throughout the city and provides artists with an opportunity to network and make music with like-minded individuals.
